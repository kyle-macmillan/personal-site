My current research focuses on enabling
data sharing using data provenance and data governance generally. In the past, 
I worked on internet measurement problems relating to video conferencing applications and speed
tests. My advisor is [Nick Feamster](https://people.cs.uchicago.edu/~feamster/). I am also fortunate 
to be mentored by [Bridget Fahey](https://www.law.uchicago.edu/faculty/fahey), [Sanjay Krishnan](https://sanjayk.io/?src=%2F~skr%2F), and 
[Raul Castro Fernandez](https://raulcastrofernandez.com/). My CV can be found [here](/pdfs/cv.pdf).
