Sidenotes are a great way to add{{< sidenote >}}You can add **formatted** content in sidenotes, including _emphasis_ and [links](https://example.com).{{< /sidenote >}} supplementary information to your content without cluttering the main narrative.

## How to Use Sidenotes

Simply use the shortcode in your markdown:

\`\`\`
{{</* sidenote */>}}Your note content here{{</* /sidenote */>}}
\`\`\`

The sidenotes will automatically number themselves{{< sidenote >}}This is the third sidenote, and it's automatically numbered!{{< /sidenote >}} and appear in the margin on desktop, or become toggleable on mobile devices.
