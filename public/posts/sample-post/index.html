How to Use Sidenotes

Simply use the shortcode in your markdown:

``` {{< sidenote >}}Your note content here{{< /sidenote >}} ```

The sidenotes will automatically number themselves This is the third sidenote, and it’s automatically numbered! and appear in the margin on desktop, or become toggleable on mobile devices.
